Hair oils

Is putting oil on your scalp and your hair is part of
your hair care regimen? Some people may think it’s quite
unnecessary since our scalp has glands producing sebum.
This oily secretion keeps the skin hydrated and forms a
protective layer on each strand of hair to waterproof it.
But that is the ideal scenario.
However, frequent washing and exposure to wind and dust strip
the skin and the hair of their natural coat of oil, making the
hair frizzy and the scalp dry, itchy, and flaky. The least we
can do is apply natural oils to keep them healthy. Unless you
have overly sensitive skin or some other specific
contraindication, regular oil massage can pamper your scalp and
keep your hair lustrous.
