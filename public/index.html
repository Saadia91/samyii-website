The Barefoot Contessa (1954)

The barefoot contessa tells the tragic tale of Maria Vargas, a Spanish dancer who shoots to stardom after being discovered at a Spanish nightclub. With the help of straight shooter Harry Dawes (Humphrey Bogart), a writer/director and her only friend and confidant, Maria goes from poverty to Hollywood stardom, but behind the success Maria feels there’s something lacking in her life. A love she’s yearned for since her days living in poverty.

About Me!

Hello! I'm Saadia, I'm 25 and I'm an aspiring front end web developer, classic movie fan and an amateur photographer based in London. Welcome to my online portfolio.

I love creating stunning visual designs that you can check out in the Side Projects section. In March 2017 I began studying web development and have spent most of my time designing and creating websites using HTML5, CSS3 and Bootstrap. I'm always looking to expand my skills and I am currently learning Javacript and Angular.
I've always been a classic movie enthusiast (I particularly enjoy 40s drama/romance and 60s horror films) and after graduating with a degree in Media and Communications, I developed a flair for studying media text. Check out my reviews and movie recommendations in the Classic Movie Review section.
